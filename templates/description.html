Main login window

Main Login Window this is the main login window.

Main window

This is the main window of the messenger.

Here you can add dialogs, enter dialogs, and view important information.

On the right are the buttons for viewing the weather, covid 19 statistics.

There is also a built-in player, through which you can listen to your music.

Main dialog window

Main dialog window is the main window for communicating with people.

There is also a panel with important information.

These are the settings.

Here you can view your data and check for updates.

Music

This is a built-in player.

Through it, you can listen to music downloaded to your computer.

Weather

This app will help you to view the weather.

You can view the weather in any city in the world, in any language.
